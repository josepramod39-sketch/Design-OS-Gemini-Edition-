# EdithRp

## Description
An intelligent backend inventory management system designed for liquor stores, using Gemini AI to automate stock tracking via invoice scanning and provide predictive insights to optimize business performance.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Manual Data Entry
Liquor stores often struggle with tedious stock updates. EdithRp automates this by analyzing images of receipts and invoices to update inventory instantly.

### Problem 2: Stock Inefficiencies
Store owners risk stockouts or dead stock. EdithRp uses "Predict and Prescribe" logic to suggest timely reorders or discounts based on sales trends.

### Problem 3: Complex Data Access
Finding specific insights is often difficult in legacy systems. EdithRp allows users to query stock levels and reports using natural voice commands.

## Key Features
- AI Invoice & Receipt Scanning (Gemini/Vertex AI)
- Predictive Reordering & Discount Suggestions
- Voice-Activated Inventory Search
- Sales & Stock Analytics Reporting
