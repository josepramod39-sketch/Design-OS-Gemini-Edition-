# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Inventory & Scanning
Manage stock levels and automatically update inventory by scanning invoices/receipts.

### 2. Analytics Dashboard
Visualize sales trends, stock turnover, and performance reports.

### 3. Predictive Insights
View AI-driven suggestions for reordering stock and applying discounts to slow-moving items.

### 4. Voice Command Center
Search for products and query stock status using natural voice commands.
